How it Works

Get started with automated trading in 4 simple steps

Quick Start Guide
1

Create a System

Go to Systems and click "Create System". Give it a name, select your broker (paper trading or real), and enable "Auto Submit" if you want trades to fill automatically.

2

Get Your Webhook URL

After creating a system, copy the webhook URL. This is where you'll send trade alerts from TradingView or other platforms.

3

Send a Trade Alert

Use our JSON builder or send a POST request with your trade details:

{ "ticker": "{{ticker}}", "action": "buy", "quantity": 1, "orderType": "MARKET", "price": "{{close}}" }

4

Monitor Your Trades

View all your trades in the Trades page. Open positions show live P&L, and you can close trades manually or let them auto-fill based on your settings.

Supported Asset Types
📈

Stocks, Crypto & Futures

Standard trading with automatic price fetching and P&L calculation. Use the standard JSON format for all three.

⚡

Options

Add contract (call/put), strike, and expiry fields to your JSON for options trading. 100x multiplier applied automatically.
