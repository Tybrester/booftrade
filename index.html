How It Works

Pick Your Product.
We Handle the Rest.

Three ways to automate your trading — each built for a different strategy.

1

Connect TradingView

Link your TradingView account and pick any indicator or strategy — built-in or community-made.

2

Create a System on BoofCapital

Name your system, choose your broker, set your position size and asset. Takes under 2 minutes.

3

Paste the Webhook URL into TradingView

Copy your unique webhook URL from BoofCapital and add it to your TradingView alert. Set the JSON payload with your buy/sell signal.

4

Orders Fire Automatically

When TradingView fires an alert, BoofCapital receives it instantly and places the order with your broker. No manual action needed.

1

Create a Bot

Choose your asset type — stocks, crypto, or futures — then select your symbol (e.g. SPY, BTC, ES) and set your dollar amount per trade.

2

Set Rules for the Bot

Pick your signal strategy (Boof 1.0–7.0), trading style — Scalper, Swing, or Position — and define your entry, exit, and risk parameters.

3

Connect Your Broker

Link tastytrade for live trading or use the built-in paper account to test your bot risk-free first.

4

Start the Bot

Flip the toggle. The bot scans the market on its interval, detects signals, and places trades automatically — 24/7.

1

Create an Options Bot

Select your underlying symbol and configure the bot to trade calls, puts, or both depending on market direction.

2

Select a System

Choose the signal system that drives your bot's entries and exits.

3

Set Rules for the Bot

Define entry and exit conditions — expiry (0DTE, weekly), delta or strike selection, profit targets, and stop losses.

4

Connect Your Broker

Options bots execute through tastytrade's live API. Authorize once and the bot handles all order routing.

5

Let the Bot Trade Options for You

The bot monitors conditions, opens and closes positions based on your rules — fully hands-off.
